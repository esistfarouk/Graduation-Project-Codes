//MOTORS DIRECTION PINS
B12 -> MOTOR RIGHT CW
B13 -> MOTOR RIGHT CCW
B14 -> MOTOR LEFT CW
B14 -> MOTOR LEFT CCW

//UART1 PINS
A9 -> RX of BURNER (TX of UART1)
A10 -> TX of BURNER (RX of UART1)

//SELECTOR PINS
A11 -> POSITION 1 SWITCH (NONE)
A12 -> POSITION 2 SWITCH (JOYSTICK)
A15 -> POSITION 3 SWITCH (EYE)
B3  -> POSITION 4 SWITCH (VOICE)
B4  -> POSITION 5 SWITCH (REMOTE CONTROL)
B5  -> POSITION 6 SWITCH (NONE)

//I2C PINS (MLX90614)
B6 -> SCL (I2C1)
B7 -> SDA (I2C1)

//POWER PINS
G -> GND of BreadBorad
3.3 -> +ve 3.3V of BreadBorad

//UART3 PINS
B11 -> TX of DEBUGGER (RX of UART3)
B10 -> RX of DEBUGGER (TX of UART3)

//MOTORS PWN PINS
A7 -> LEFT MOTOR PWM (TIM3 Channel2)
A6 -> RIGHT MOTOR PWM (TIM2 Channel1)

//UART2 PINS
A3 -> RX of Raspberry pi (TX of UART2)
A2 -> TX of Raspberry pi (RX of UART2)

//ADC PINS (JOYSTICK)
A0 -> JoyStick Y (ADC1 Channel0)
A1 -> JoyStick X (ADC1 Channel1)
